Dark Restaurant Nocti Vagus

Nocti Vagus invites you to an exceptional culinary experience that will trigger your senses. In total darkness you will be served various high quality dishes, escorted by blind waiters.

Not being able to see means your other senses have to work for it, one’s ability to taste and smell food sharpens. There is so much to be ‘’seen’’ when eyes are closed.

Dining at Nocti Vagus in complete darkness is one of the two outstanding experiences offered to our guests. We offer an unique experience of dining and sensual theater in the dark, experiencing art, music and acting like you have never done before.

We offer high quality culinary dishes and pre-planned meal orders.

Nocti Vagus have highly trained blind or visually impaired waiters and guides that will lead you safely through the whole experience.

Experience art, music and acting in a completely new sensual setting.
