About us

Berlins first dark restuarant and theatre

For over 10 years we have served satisfied guests in Berlin in the city first Dark Restaurant. The idea was created in 1993, where the conceptual idea was born in Magdeburg. The idea was originally focused on purely culinary experiences, but the interest in working with and offering job to those with a vulnerability showed to be bigger than expected. Nocti Vagus also wanted to offer an unique experience unlike other culinary experiences.

The two ideas of building a workplace for people with a vulnerability and looking to offer Nocti Vagus guests a surprising experience collided into the concept of dining in the dark and became Berlin’s first Dark Restaurant.



We experienced people enjoying and tasting food completely different without being able to use their eyes, so we wished to continue experimenting with this, so in 1999 we combined the culinary experience in the dark and we built the world’s first dark theater stage, where we wished to really make people experience a sensual adventure unlike any other. Hearing, feeling and sensing actors performing.

Today Nocti Vagus is one of Berlin's most successful niche restaurants with some of the best trained guides who are blind or severely visually impaired and will lead you safely through the experience. We offer our team a special training so we can offer the best service for our guests.

For this high quality Nocti Vagus has been honoured several times by the The Golden Service crown, the Gastro Award and by the German Vegetarian Society.
